Rafting Travel

Rafting Travel Company is dedicated to providing exhilarating and safe white water rafting experiences that connect adventurers with the natural beauty of Idaho's rivers. Our mission is to create unforgettable memories through expertly guided trips, emphasizing environmental stewardship, safety, and a deep appreciation for the great outdoors.

History

Rafting Travel Company was founded in 1998 by a group of passionate river enthusiasts eager to share their love for Idaho's stunning waterways. Starting with just a few rafts, we have grown into one of the premier rafting companies in the region, introducing thousands of adventurers to the thrills of white water rafting while always prioritizing safety and a deep respect for nature. Our founders, with decades of rafting experience and extensive knowledge of Idaho's rivers, ensured each trip is led by expert guides who are skilled navigators and dedicated conservationists. We have built a reputation for excellence, with many guests returning year after year to explore new sections of river and create lasting memories. As we look to the future, Rafting Travel Company remains committed to providing high-quality, sustainable rafting adventures that showcase the best of Idaho's wild and scenic rivers.
