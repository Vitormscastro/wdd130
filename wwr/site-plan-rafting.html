Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share information about our company. It should clearly display information about time, location and how to schedule. It should display an easy way to make reservations for people who already have a group and want to set up their event.

Audience

The target audience are people in a family going through their later years as a family. Parents and their teenage or adult children interested in outdoor activities and sports. They should be able to access the site mainly through their phones. The site should meet the family's needs of having an excelent place to hold their family activities to strengthen their bonds and relationships.
